quantatitive financial stock market analysis

This article provides an overview of popular Python libraries and frameworks for stock market analysis, trading software development, and high-frequency/low latency trading. It covers tools such as Altreva Adaptive Modeler, fms, MyTT, funcat, stable-baselines3, crypto trading bots, qlib, FinRL, OpenBBTerminal, Zipline, and Pyalgotrade, which can be used for tasks like data preprocessing, algorithm development, backtesting, and live trading. The article aims to help developers choose the right tool or combination of tools based on their specific needs.
